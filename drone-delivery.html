"Drone Delivery"

Logo

1). Elements:
Letter “D” that stands for “Delivery” and “Drone” to emphasize that these two are possible.
2). Fonts and Forms:
The font is “Squares Bold” to make focus on technology and Know How.
The part of drone is visualised with simple and rounded curves to give it a light and dynamic look.
3). Colors:
dark Blue and Light Blue to transfer an association with Flying and Sky. For visit cards I’ve implemented also contrast Yellow & Pink to give a brave and innovative touch.

Key Visual

The comparison of two workers of post-office: now and with Drone Delivery. The worker, that controls drone, looks more self-confident and happier, than the “classic” one.

Creative Idea Implementation

Communication Task is to switch from classic way of delivery to a new one.
Communication channel: Business centres, offices of potential clients.
Communication instrument: Billboard, leaflet directly addressed to the companies post-box, personalized mail-delivery etc.
