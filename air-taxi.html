"Air Taxi"

Logo

1) Elements:
 1)The Lion, as a king of animals visualising the potential customer, capable to afford a premium-class service.
2)Taxi squares
3) Wings - to communicate the Flying. Lion looks a bit agressive, so that snobby and self-confident to make it familiar with the potential Target Group.
2) Fonts and Forms:
Font is “Zoela Demo” to give communication a luxurious touch. Forms are smooth, but exquisite to stay premium.
3) Colors:
Primary colors: Golden or Silver (depending on background) to deliver a luxurious look. Secondary: White or Black

Key Visual

KV interpretates the logo. The Flying lion at night looks a bit mysterious and not for everyone affordable.

Creative Idea Implementation

Communication task is to switch consumers from classic Taxi to Air Taxi by maneuvering with their EGO.
Communication channel: Billboard through the highway or just on overloaded traffic areas (where the need to be fast is mostly actual)
Communication instrument: Billboards, magazines etc.
