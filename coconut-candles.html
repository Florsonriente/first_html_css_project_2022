"Coconut candles"

Logo

1). Elements:
As I wanted to put the woman (key consumer) and the Nature on the equal level, I’ve illustrated in the form of burning candle two sides of it: a woman with long hair (at the right) and a tree (at the left).

Together they look like a candle light. They are standing in a coconut, that makes direct connection with coconut wax.

2). Fonts and Forms:
The font is “Typolino”, which is familiar to the category, looks feminine and luxurious.
Forms are smooth and in some areas too thin to deliver more exoticism that speaks for coconut origin.

3). Colors:
natural colors, but not too bright or strong to deliver the association with Relax.

Key Visual

The woman and Nature are the one and only. The woman looks relaxed and enjoying. The exotic leaves and beautiful flowers are making the entire universe together. I’ve implemented some geometric and cutting effects to make it look brave, “behind the rules” and attractive for young audience.

Creative Idea Implementation

Communication task is to switch the users from usual candles to a coconut by increasing the awareness of their harmfullness for the nevironment.
Communication channel: Magazines, Billboards, Web campaign.
Communication instrument: Posters, Branded products for self-care, SPA.
