"Flavoured Coffee"

Logo



1) Elements:
I wanted to stay within the category, that’s why I’ve illustrated the cup of coffee (what is typical for the category) and a hand to deliver the “To Go” option.
Second element is a Aroma Palette Wheel: there are 6 types of Flavours can be added into coffee. (Aroma Wheel)

2) Fonts and Forms:
to stay in the category, I used two Fonts
1) “Coffee Milkshake” to be familiar with product
2) “Lovely Coffee” to communicate the Naturality of Ingredients. Forms are smooth, but very simple as this logo is targeted on mass consumer.

3) Colors:
I made a decision to create the colorful logo to play with a contrast on usual brown color and the variety of colors&tastes that can be added to it. I chose green for “Flavoured” to make focus on Naturality.

Key Visual

KV visualises the key Ingredient of the Season, the coffee is prepared with. I’ve used “Hand Made” way to put the accent on Naturality and to address the young Audience.

Creative Idea Implementation

Communication task is 1)to switch buyers from HoReCa channel that buy classic coffee
2)to source buyers of BIO products from usual Supermarkts by focusing on key seasonal Ingredient added into Coffee.
Communication channel: Ground branding, Stands near the entrance, Branding in Supermarkts in BIO category
Communication instrument: Branded Menu, A3 Posters, Wobler.
